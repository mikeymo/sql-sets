SQL Sets Software Support

General Support & Questions

SQL Sets strives to provide highly responsive and accurate support to its customers. If you have a general support question, please send an email, with as much detail as necessary, to support@sqlsets.com. We will answer your question as promptly as possible and help you get on your way. We generally respond to all customer support requests within 1 business day.

Bug Reporting

If you would like to report a bug related to SQL Sets, please send a detailed email to bugreport@sqlsets.com that includes the following information:

  1. Detailed explanation of what you were doing at the time the bug occurred
  2. Copy of the error message and/or exception
  3. Screenshot if applicable
  4. What version of Windows (including Service Pack) you are running
