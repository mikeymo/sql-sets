About SQL Sets

Overview

SQL Sets is product developed by PrimalBits Software, a privately held company in East Hampton, CT. It's core benefit/feature is to allow developers, DBAs, QA Analysts and business partners to easily share and collaborate on SQL Server data. In providing this functionality, it also includes many other features that facilitate extracting data from SQL Server and working with that data.

Like most software, SQL Sets grew out of a real business need faced by information technology professionals every day. Company founder and SQL Sets developer, Mike Morris, has worked for and consulted in many different companies for close to 20 years. In that time he has cut and pasted SQL Server data countless times into emails, Word documents and Excel spreadsheets. This certainly worked, but the process was cumbersome and the sharing and collaboration experience was awkward at best. Thus, SQL Sets was born. Our goal is to continue to improve SQL Sets so that sharing and working with your data in SQL Sets becomes a natural extension to the way you do your job every day.
